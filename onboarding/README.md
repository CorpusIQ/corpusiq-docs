# Onboarding

Welcome to CorpusIQ. Here's how to get your team up and running.

## Individual Setup (5 Minutes)

1. [Sign up](https://corpusiq.io) — Free 30-day trial, no credit card
2. [Connect your first data source](../quick-start.md) — One click OAuth
3. [Link your AI tool](../quick-start.md#3-connect-your-ai-tool) — ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity
4. Ask your first question

## Team Setup

For teams, we recommend:

1. **Admin connects core data sources** — Accounting, CRM, analytics, ecommerce
2. **Team members connect individual tools** — Email, calendar, project management
3. **Set up Slack or Teams** — Team members can query from chat

## Common First Questions

- "What was our revenue last month?"
- "Show me open HubSpot deals over $10k"
- "Which marketing channels had the best ROAS?"
- "Pull up the latest QuickBooks P&L"
