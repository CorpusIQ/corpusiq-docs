# Outlook

## What it unlocks
Pull your Outlook inbox and calendar into the conversation. Ask what's on your plate this week, what a customer last emailed you, or what's blocking a deal — without opening Outlook.

## Before you connect
- A Microsoft 365 or Outlook.com account.
- About 1 minute. If you already connected OneDrive under Microsoft 365, Outlook may already be authorized.

## How to connect
1. Open your CorpusIQ dashboard and click Connections.
2. Find Outlook (or Microsoft 365) and click Connect.
<!-- screenshot: Microsoft connection card -->
3. Sign in with your Microsoft account.
4. Review the requested permissions (Mail.Read, Calendars.Read) and click Accept.
<!-- screenshot: Microsoft permission consent screen -->
5. You'll return to CorpusIQ.

You'll see Outlook change from gray to green in your CorpusIQ dashboard.

## What CorpusIQ can see
- Your Outlook emails: list, search, and read full content.
- Mailbox structure: folders, message counts, unread count.
- Your Outlook calendars and upcoming events.
- Event details and search across your calendar.

Read-only. CorpusIQ never sends mail, replies, deletes messages, or creates events.

## Questions you can ask
- "What's on my calendar this week?"
- "Find the last email from Acme Corp."
- "Did anyone email me about the lease renewal?"
- "Summarize this morning's unread mail."

## Troubleshooting
- "Admin approval required" — your IT admin must whitelist CorpusIQ in Microsoft Entra. Send them the consent URL from your dashboard.
- Calendar empty — confirm the events are in your default calendar, not a shared/team calendar.
- "Token expired" — Microsoft tokens roll over often. Click Reconnect in your dashboard.
