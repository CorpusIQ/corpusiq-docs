# YouTube

## What it unlocks
Bring your YouTube channel analytics into conversation alongside your other marketing data. Ask which videos drove traffic, where viewers are coming from, and what's working — without bouncing into YouTube Studio.

## Before you connect
- A YouTube channel (personal or Brand Account).
- A Google account that owns or manages the channel.
- About 2 minutes. If you already connected Google Workspace, YouTube may already be authorized under the same Google connection.

## How to connect
1. Open your CorpusIQ dashboard and click Connections.
2. Find YouTube and click Connect.
<!-- screenshot: YouTube card on the connections page -->
3. Sign into your Google account.
4. Pick the channel you want to connect (important if you manage Brand Accounts).
<!-- screenshot: Google account/channel picker -->
5. Click Allow on the consent screen.

You'll see YouTube change from gray to green in your CorpusIQ dashboard.

## What CorpusIQ can see
- Your channel: subscriber count, total views, video count.
- Your uploaded videos and per-video analytics (views, watch time, likes).
- Geographic breakdown of your viewers.
- Transcripts and comments for any public video.
- General YouTube search results.
- Brand Account channels you own or manage.

Read-only. CorpusIQ never uploads, edits videos, replies to comments, or changes channel settings.

## Questions you can ask
- "What are my top YouTube videos this quarter?"
- "Where are most of my YouTube viewers from geographically?"
- "Show me the transcript of my latest upload."
- "How many subscribers did I gain last month?"
- "What are people saying in the comments of my newest video?"

## Troubleshooting
- "Channel not found" — Brand Accounts need an extra picker step. Disconnect and reconnect, then choose the correct channel.
- Analytics empty — YouTube Analytics has a ~48-hour lag for fresh data.
- Permission error on a Brand Account — your Google login must have at least Manager access on the Brand Account.
