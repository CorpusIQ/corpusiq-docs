# Connect Airtable to Claude (Ask Your Databases in Plain English)

Airtable holds your operational data — projects, inventory, CRM, content calendars. Connect it to Claude through CorpusIQ and ask questions instead of building views and filters manually.

## What you can ask once connected

- "Show me all projects due this week that are still in progress."
- "Which inventory items are below reorder threshold?"
- "How many leads did we add to our CRM base this month?"
- "Find all content pieces scheduled for next week without a writer assigned."
- "Which client projects are over budget?"

## What CorpusIQ reads from Airtable

- All bases you have access to
- All tables and records within those bases
- Field values, linked records, and attachment names
- Filtered and sorted record sets

Read-only. CorpusIQ never modifies your Airtable bases or records.
