# Mailchimp

## What it unlocks
Pull your Mailchimp campaign performance into the same conversation as your store revenue, ad spend, and customer list. Ask whether your last newsletter actually drove sales — not just opens.

## Before you connect
- A Mailchimp account with admin access.
- The audience (list) you want analyzed should already exist.
- About 2 minutes.

## How to connect
1. Open your CorpusIQ dashboard and click Connections.
2. Find Mailchimp in the list and click Connect.
<!-- screenshot: CorpusIQ connections page with Mailchimp row highlighted -->
3. You'll be redirected to Mailchimp. Log in if prompted.
4. Click Allow on the Mailchimp authorization screen.
<!-- screenshot: Mailchimp "Allow access" consent screen -->
5. You'll land back in CorpusIQ.

You'll see Mailchimp change from gray to green in your CorpusIQ dashboard.

## What CorpusIQ can see
- Your campaigns and their send/open/click rates.
- Your audiences (lists) with subscriber counts.
- Per-campaign reports including bounces and unsubscribes.

Read-only. CorpusIQ never sends mail, edits campaigns, or changes your lists.

## Questions you can ask
- "How did last week's Mailchimp campaign perform?"
- "Which of my recent campaigns had the highest click rate?"
- "Show me my Mailchimp audience growth."
- "Compare my Mailchimp opens to Shopify revenue on the same days."

## Troubleshooting
- "Authorization failed" — your Mailchimp session expired. Log into Mailchimp in another tab, then retry Connect.
- No campaigns appear — confirm the campaigns are in Sent status, not Draft.
- Wrong audience showing — Mailchimp accounts can have multiple audiences. Specify the audience name in your question.
