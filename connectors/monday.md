# Monday.com

## What it unlocks
Bring your Monday.com boards into conversations about how the business is actually running. Ask which projects are blocked, who owns what, and what's slipping — without opening the app.

## Before you connect
- A Monday.com account with access to the boards you care about.
- About 2 minutes.

## How to connect
1. Open your CorpusIQ dashboard and click Connections.
2. Find Monday.com in the list and click Connect.
<!-- screenshot: Connections page with Monday.com row -->
3. Log into Monday.com if you aren't already.
4. Click Authorize on the consent screen.
<!-- screenshot: Monday.com OAuth authorize screen -->
5. You'll return to CorpusIQ.

You'll see Monday.com change from gray to green in your CorpusIQ dashboard.

## What CorpusIQ can see
- Your workspaces and boards.
- Board structure: columns, groups, and items (rows/pulses).
- Item details: status, owners, dates, and column values.

Read-only. CorpusIQ never changes statuses, assigns items, or edits boards.

## Questions you can ask
- "What Monday.com tasks are overdue?"
- "Show me everything assigned to Sarah this week."
- "Which projects are blocked right now?"
- "Summarize the Q1 roadmap board."

## Troubleshooting
- Board not showing up — confirm your Monday user has at least View access on that board.
- "No items returned" on a large board — narrow the question to a specific group or status.
- Auth fails — reset the Monday connector in your dashboard and reconnect.
