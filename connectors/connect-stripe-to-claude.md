# Connect Stripe to Claude (Ask Your Payments in Plain English)

Stripe holds your revenue data. Claude has the reasoning. CorpusIQ connects them so you can ask plain-English questions about your payments, payouts, and disputes.

## What you can ask once connected

- "How much revenue did we process this month vs last month?"
- "Which customers have the highest lifetime spend?"
- "Show me all failed payments in the last 7 days."
- "What is our refund rate this quarter?"
- "Are there any open disputes I need to respond to?"

## What CorpusIQ reads from Stripe

- Charges and payments
- Customers and lifetime value
- Payouts and balance transactions
- Refunds
- Disputes and chargebacks
- Current balance (available and pending)

Read-only. CorpusIQ never initiates charges or modifies your Stripe account.

## Connect Stripe to Claude in 2 minutes

1. Sign up at corpusiq.io
2. Click Connections then Stripe then enter your restricted API key
3. Ask your first question
