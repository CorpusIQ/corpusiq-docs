# Connect Perplexity to Your Business Data via MCP

Perplexity is built for real-time information. Combine it with your actual business data via CorpusIQ MCP and you get an AI that answers questions about the world AND your business simultaneously.

## What changes when you connect

Before: "What is the average SaaS churn rate?" gives you a generic industry answer.
After: "What is our churn rate vs industry average and which customer segment is dragging it down?" gives you your live data plus world knowledge in one answer.

## What you can ask Perplexity once connected

- "How does our ROAS compare to industry benchmarks? Pull our actual numbers."
- "What is the current Google algorithm update and how does it affect our Search Console traffic?"
- "Are our Klaviyo open rates above or below current industry averages?"

## How to connect

1. Sign up at corpusiq.io
2. Connect your tools via OAuth (Shopify, QuickBooks, Google Ads, etc.)
3. Add the CorpusIQ MCP server to Perplexity
4. Ask questions that combine your data with world knowledge
