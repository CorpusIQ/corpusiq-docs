# AI for Legal Firms — Document Management, Billing, Case Analysis

Law firms juggle multiple data silos: document management systems, billing and time-tracking software, CRM platforms, email, and case management databases. Traditionally, answering a cross-system question — like „Which matters are over budget this quarter and have outstanding invoices?" — means checking three different systems and manually correlating the results. **AI for legal firms** through CorpusIQ MCP collapses the silos: connect your document management, billing, CRM, and databases to ChatGPT or Claude, and get answers across your entire practice in plain English, from live data, with full source citations.

No system-hopping. No manual correlation. One question, instant answer, fully attributable.

## Security: Confidentiality by Design

| Protection | Implementation |
|---|---|
| **Read-Only** | OAuth scopes limited to read — ChatGPT cannot modify documents, change billing entries, or alter client records |
| **No Data Storage** | Zero persistence — client data is queried live, never stored or cached |
| **Encryption** | TLS 1.3 with forward secrecy — all data encrypted in transit |
| **Access Inheritance** | Your existing DMS/CRM/billing user permissions govern what each connection can access |
| **No AI Training** | Client data and legal documents are never used to train machine learning models |
| **Ephemeral Answers** | Responses exist only for the duration of the query — nothing persists |

This architecture supports the confidentiality and ethical obligations of legal practice. No client data leaves its source system. No documents are copied to external databases. The AI sees data only for the moment of the question.
