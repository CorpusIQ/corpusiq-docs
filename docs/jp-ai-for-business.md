# AI for Japanese Business — 日本のビジネスにAIを

Japanese businesses operate in one of the world's most sophisticated yet demanding markets: strict compliance requirements (個人情報保護法), multi-currency complexity (JPY, USD, EUR), a unique accounting ecosystem, and the need to bridge domestic operations with global markets. **AI for Japanese business** through CorpusIQ MCP connects your tools to ChatGPT or Claude — ask questions in 日本語 or English, and get live, source-cited answers without moving or storing any data.

No exports. No SQL. No privacy concerns. Just answers from your actual business data, instantly.

---

## Why Japanese Businesses Need AI Now

| Challenge | The Old Way | With CorpusIQ MCP |
|---|---|---|
| **Multi-Currency Reporting** | Export data from each system, manually convert to JPY in Excel | 「全チャネルの今月の売上を円換算で表示して」 — answered in seconds |
| **Departmental Reporting** | Run separate reports by 部門 (division), consolidate manually | 「部門別の経費を前年同期と比較して」 |
| **Cross-Border Consolidation** | Email subsidiaries for reports, reconcile in spreadsheets | Query Japan HQ + overseas subsidiary data simultaneously |
| **Cash Flow Monitoring** | Compile bank balances, receivables, payables across systems | 「連結ベースのネットキャッシュポジションを表示」 |
| **Compliance & Audit** | Manually extract data for 税務調査 (tax audit) preparation | Query live transaction history with source citations instantly |
