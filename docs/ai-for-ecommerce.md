# AI for Ecommerce — Shopify, Amazon, eBay Analytics Connected to ChatGPT

Ecommerce teams juggle multiple channels — Shopify for DTC, Amazon Seller Central for marketplace sales, eBay for auctions and fixed-price listings. Each platform has its own analytics dashboard, its own metrics, and its own reporting format. **AI for ecommerce** through CorpusIQ MCP collapses the chaos: connect all your sales channels to ChatGPT, ask questions in plain English, and get live, cross-channel answers sourced from your actual store data.

No CSV exports. No dashboard hopping. No reconciling numbers across tabs. One question, one answer, all your channels.
