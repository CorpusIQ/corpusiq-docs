# AI for Small Business — Affordable Intelligence for 1-50 Person Teams

Small businesses run on the same tools as enterprises — QuickBooks for accounting, Shopify for ecommerce, HubSpot for CRM. The difference? Small teams don't have data engineers, BI analysts, or six-figure analytics budgets. **AI for small business** bridges that gap: connect your existing tools to ChatGPT or Claude through CorpusIQ MCP, and get live, source-cited answers in plain English — for a fraction of what enterprise BI costs.

No data warehouse. No SQL. No IT team required. Just answers from your own data, in seconds.

## The Small Business Data Problem

Most small businesses sit on a goldmine of data spread across 5-15 SaaS tools — but accessing it means:

| Workflow | Time Cost |
|---|---|
| Log into QuickBooks, generate a P&L report, export to CSV | 5-10 minutes |
| Log into Shopify, pull order data, export | 5 minutes |
| Log into HubSpot, check pipeline, export deals | 5 minutes |
| Combine all three in Excel, build pivot tables | 20-40 minutes |
| Answer a single cross-tool question | **30-60 minutes** |

For a founder or small-team operator, 30-60 minutes per question means most questions never get asked. Decisions get made on gut feel instead of data.

**AI for small business changes the math from 30 minutes to 30 seconds.**

## Security: Built for Business Owners Who Can't Afford Mistakes

| Security Layer | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Read-only by default** | The AI can *read* your QuickBooks — it cannot create, modify, or delete anything |
| **OAuth 2.0** | No shared passwords, no API keys stored in plaintext |
| **TLS 1.3** | Encryption in transit with forward secrecy |
| **No data storage** | CorpusIQ does not store, cache, or retain your business data after queries complete |
| **User-scoped isolation** | Each team member's data is completely isolated — no cross-account access |

For small business owners, this means zero risk of the AI accidentally modifying financial records, customer data, or inventory.

<details>
<summary><strong>Do I need technical skills to use AI for my small business?</strong></summary>

No. CorpusIQ setup requires only OAuth authentication — clicking "Connect" and logging into each platform. If you can log into QuickBooks, you can connect it. Asking questions requires no SQL, no coding, just plain English.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How much does CorpusIQ cost for a small business?</strong></summary>

CorpusIQ offers a free 30-day trial with full access to all connectors. Paid plans are priced for SMBs — comparable to a single SaaS subscription. Visit [corpusiq.io](https://www.corpusiq.io) for current pricing. No per-query charges, no data volume fees, no hidden costs.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can the AI accidentally delete or modify my data?</strong></summary>

No. All CorpusIQ connectors use read-only OAuth scopes. The AI can only query and report — it cannot create, update, delete, or modify any business data. This is enforced at the authentication layer, not by a policy.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if I only use one or two tools? Is AI still useful?</strong></summary>

Absolutely. Even with just QuickBooks connected, asking "What were my top 5 expense categories last quarter?" saves 10-15 minutes over running reports manually. The value compounds as you add more tools.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How is this different from using ChatGPT directly?</strong></summary>

ChatGPT without MCP cannot access your actual business data. It answers from its training data — general knowledge, not your specific numbers. With CorpusIQ, ChatGPT queries your live QuickBooks, Shopify, and CRM data and returns answers based on your real records.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can my whole team use it?</strong></summary>

Yes. Each team member creates their own CorpusIQ account and connects their own tools. This preserves individual OAuth permissions — each person sees only the data their credentials allow. For growing teams, the flat plan pricing means adding users doesn't multiply costs.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if I grow beyond 50 people?</strong></summary>

CorpusIQ scales with you. The same MCP platform supports both small teams and enterprises. As your business grows, you can add more connectors, enable SSO, and access enterprise features without switching platforms.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Does this work on mobile?</strong></summary>

Yes. Any MCP-compatible AI client — including ChatGPT's mobile app — can query your connected business data. Check revenue during your morning commute without opening a laptop.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What business tools are supported?</strong></summary>

37+ native connectors including QuickBooks, Xero, HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, Amazon Seller Central, Stripe, Google Analytics, Meta Ads, Google Ads, Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Slack, Gmail, Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, Notion, PostgreSQL, MSSQL, MongoDB, and more. New connectors are added regularly.
