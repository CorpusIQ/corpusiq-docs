## The Dashboard Trap

Polar Analytics is good at what it does: pre-built ecommerce dashboards. But here's the trap:

You connect your data sources. You get beautiful charts. You spend 20 minutes clicking through tabs. And you still haven't answered the one question that matters: **"What should I do?"**

A dashboard shows you that revenue dipped 12%. It doesn't tell you which SKU is burning ad spend and needs to be paused. It doesn't reconcile the conflicting numbers from Meta and Shopify. It leaves the decision to you — after you've exported to CSV and built a pivot table.

CorpusIQ answers the question directly.
