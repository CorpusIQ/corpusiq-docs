## The Northbeam Problem

Northbeam is powerful. It builds sophisticated multi-touch attribution models. But here's the reality for most operators:

1. **Setup takes weeks.** You need to instrument every touchpoint, configure the model, and wait for enough data.
2. **You're still modeling, not reconciling.** An attribution model is an educated guess. Cross-source reconciliation is fact-checking.
3. **It's expensive.** Northbeam starts at ~$500/month. Most Shopify operators don't need — or can't justify — enterprise attribution.

## Why CorpusIQ Is Different

CorpusIQ doesn't model attribution. It **reconciles** it.

When Meta says you made $10,000 and Shopify says you made $8,200, Northbeam builds a model to guess who's right. CorpusIQ reads both APIs, matches transactions, and shows you the gap.

It's the difference between "our model estimates..." and "here's exactly where the $1,800 discrepancy is."
