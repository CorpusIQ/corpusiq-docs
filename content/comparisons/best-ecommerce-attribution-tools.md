## Why Cross-Source Attribution Matters

Every platform claims credit for conversions differently:

- **Meta** uses view-through attribution (someone saw your ad, bought later = Meta claims the sale)
- **Shopify** uses last-click (the last thing they clicked before buying)
- **Google Ads** uses its own data-driven model
- **Klaviyo** attributes revenue to email flows

They will never agree. The only way to know what's actually working is to reconcile them — match transactions across platforms and calculate true ROAS.
