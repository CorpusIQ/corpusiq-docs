# Indie Hackers Post: I Stopped Building Dashboards. Now I Just Ask.

**Group:** SaaS, Automation, AI Tools

---

I spent 3 years building a dashboard company. Every customer said the same thing:

"We built 47 dashboards. Nobody looks at any of them."

The problem isn't the dashboard. It's the model. Dashboards answer pre-built questions. Real business questions chain across tools and evolve minute to minute.

So I built something different. Instead of dashboards, you connect your tools to an AI assistant once and ask whatever you want.

Connect QuickBooks, Stripe, Shopify, HubSpot, GA4 — 37+ tools. Each takes 30 seconds via OAuth. All read-only.

Then ask:

> "What's our revenue this month vs last month?"

> "Which customers haven't ordered in 60 days?"

> "What's actual ROAS across Meta and Google combined?"

Live data. Every time. No exports. No pivot tables.

I'm looking for beta users who want to try this. If you run a business and toggle between 3+ tools daily to answer questions, I'd love your feedback.
