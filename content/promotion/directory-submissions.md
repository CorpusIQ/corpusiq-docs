# Product Hunt Launch Prep

**Tagline:** Connect your business data to ChatGPT in 5 minutes. Stop building reports.
**Description:** CorpusIQ connects 37+ business tools (QuickBooks, Stripe, Shopify, HubSpot, GA4, Klaviyo) to AI assistants via MCP. Read-only OAuth. Single config entry. Ask questions in plain English and get answers from live data — no dashboards, no exports, no waiting.

**Maker comment for launch day:**
Hey Product Hunt! I built CorpusIQ after spending 3 years watching operators toggle between 5+ dashboards daily, exporting CSVs, and building reports nobody reads.

The problem: your data lives in QuickBooks, Stripe, Shopify, HubSpot, GA4, and a dozen other tools. Answering "how are we doing?" means checking all of them. Exporting. Reconciling. It's 45 minutes of data entry before you get to any actual thinking.

CorpusIQ connects your existing tools to ChatGPT via MCP. You auth once (read-only, 30 seconds per tool), then ask:

- "What's our revenue this month vs last month?"
- "Which customers haven't ordered in 60 days?"
- "What's our blended ROAS across all ad platforms?"

Live data. Every time. No reports to maintain. No spreadsheets to build.

The whole thing is read-only — the AI can't modify anything, just read and answer. 37+ connectors. Free trial, no credit card. I'd love your feedback.
