# THE OPERATOR'S VISIBILITY CHECKLIST
## How to Know If Your Business Data Is Lying to You
### Score 1-10 on each. If you score below 7 on any three, you have a visibility problem.

---

## SECTION 1: DATA INTEGRITY

- [ ] **Single-source revenue number.** When you ask "what's our revenue this month," you get ONE number. Not three different numbers from Shopify, Meta, and your ERP.
- [ ] **Cross-platform reconciliation.** Marketing says $100K in ad spend generated $400K in revenue. Your bank account says $280K. You know why.
- [ ] **No manual spreadsheet reconciliation.** If someone on your team is building pivot tables every Monday to reconcile platforms, you have broken visibility.
- [ ] **Attribution isn't guesswork.** You can trace a customer from ad click → site visit → purchase → fulfillment without gaps.

**Your score: __/4**

---

## SECTION 2: REPORTING SPEED

- [ ] **Question-to-answer under 60 seconds.** When the board asks "what's our CAC by channel," you have it in under a minute. Not "I'll get back to you."
- [ ] **No report-request bottleneck.** Your team doesn't wait on one person to pull numbers. Anyone can get the answer.
- [ ] **Real-time isn't "updated this morning."** Your dashboards show what's happening right now, not what happened yesterday.

**Your score: __/3**

---

## SECTION 3: DECISION CONFIDENCE

- [ ] **Ad spend decisions are data-backed.** You don't "feel like" a campaign is working. You know exactly which ads convert and which burn cash.
- [ ] **No Monday morning spreadsheet anxiety.** You don't dread opening Excel on Monday to find out what actually happened.
- [ ] **Board meetings run on live data.** You don't prepare reports for the board. You open the system and discuss reality.
- [ ] **Forecasting isn't fiction.** Your projections are based on actual trends across all systems, not gut feel multiplied by hope.

**Your score: __/4**

---

## SECTION 4: SYSTEMS CONNECTED

- [ ] **Shopify/Magento/WooCommerce** connected and live
- [ ] **Meta Ads / Google Ads / TikTok Ads** connected with cost + conversion data
- [ ] **Email platform** (Klaviyo/Mailchimp) connected with revenue attribution
- [ ] **ERP/Accounting** connected for true financial reconciliation
- [ ] **CRM** connected for pipeline visibility
- [ ] **All systems reconcile against each other** — no floating numbers

**Your score: __/6**

---

## SCORING

| Total Score | Visibility Level |
|:---:|-------|
| 15-17 | **Elite** — Your data tells one story |
| 12-14 | **Competent** — Some blind spots |
| 8-11 | **At Risk** — Significant visibility gaps |
| Below 8 | **Blind** — Flying without instruments |

---

## If you scored below 12:

You're making decisions with partial information. The gap between what you think is happening and what's actually happening is costing you — in wasted ad spend, missed revenue, and slow decisions.

The fix: connect all data sources. Reconcile the numbers. Make every decision from one version of the truth.
