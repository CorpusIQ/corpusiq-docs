# MCP for Supply Chain — Track Inventory, Orders, and Logistics in One Question

Supply chain data lives in Shopify (orders), your ERP (inventory), your 3PL (shipping), QuickBooks (costs), and Gmail (vendor communications).

Five systems. Zero unified view. And a stockout costs you thousands before anyone notices.

## The supply chain data problem

Supply chain managers track:

- **Shopify/Amazon:** Order volume, sell-through rates
- **ERP/Inventory system:** Stock levels, reorder points, lead times
- **3PL/Shipping:** Fulfillment status, delivery times, exceptions
- **QuickBooks:** COGS, landed costs, vendor payments
- **Gmail:** Vendor communications, PO confirmations

Answering "are we about to stock out?" requires data from all five. By the time you compile it, you've already stocked out.

## How MCP connects your supply chain

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "Which products are below reorder point? Show me current stock, weekly sell-through, and lead time."

> "What's our total inventory value? Break it down by warehouse and product category."

> "Show me all purchase orders with vendors. Which are past due?"

> "What's our landed cost per unit for top 10 products? Are margins holding?"

> "Flag any orders with shipping delays. Show me the customer and order value."

## Read-only means safe

Every connection is read-only. The AI can query inventory levels but can't create POs. It can check order status but can't modify shipments. You stay in control.
