# MCP Benefits for Business — Why MCP Matters for Operators

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is the open standard for connecting AI to business tools. Here's what that actually means for the people running companies.

## Before MCP: the data fragmentation tax

Every business operator pays the same tax: toggling between tools to answer simple questions.

"Did we have a good month?" → check QuickBooks for P&L, Stripe for revenue, Shopify for orders. Export. Reconcile. Three tools, 45 minutes.

"Which campaigns are working?" → check Meta Ads, Google Ads, Klaviyo. Export. Combine. Calculate ROAS. Three tools, 45 minutes.

"Should we hire someone?" → check QuickBooks for runway, Stripe for revenue trend, HubSpot for pipeline. Three tools, 45 minutes.

The tax is paid in time. Every day. By every operator.

## After MCP: ask and answered

Connect your tools once. Then:

> "Did we have a good month?" — answered in 15 seconds from live QuickBooks + Stripe + Shopify data.

> "Which campaigns are working?" — answered with blended ROAS from Meta + Google + Klaviyo.

> "Should we hire?" — answered with runway calculation from live financials + pipeline data.

Same questions. Same data. Zero exports. Zero reconciliation.

## The benefits, quantified

| Before MCP | After MCP |
