## Small business, same data problem

Small businesses have the same fragmented data as enterprises. The difference: no dedicated analysts to pull it all together.

**The owner does everything:** You check QuickBooks for cash position. Stripe for daily revenue. Your email for customer inquiries. Your calendar for today's appointments. Four places, four answers, zero unified view.

**Every report is manual:** Monthly P&L? Export from QuickBooks. Customer list? Export from your CRM. Cash flow forecast? Build a spreadsheet and hope the formulas are right.

**Decisions are gut-driven:** Without easy access to your own numbers, you make decisions based on feeling. "I think last month was good." "I think we can afford that hire."
