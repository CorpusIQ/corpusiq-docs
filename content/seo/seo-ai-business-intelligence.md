# AI for Business Intelligence — The Complete 2026 Guide

Business Intelligence is changing. The old model — dashboards, reports, data warehouses, dedicated analysts — is giving way to something simpler: ask a question, get an answer from live data.

Here's what's happening and what it means for operators.

## The old BI model is breaking

Three forces are killing traditional BI:

1. **Dashboards have 1.3 views/month.** Nobody checks them. They're monuments to questions someone had six months ago.

2. **Data is stale before it's queried.** The warehouse syncs every 4 hours. The CEO's question can't wait.

3. **Analysts are expensive and scarce.** Every company needs one. Most can't afford a good one.

## The new model: conversational BI

1. Connect your tools via MCP (30 seconds each, OAuth, read-only)
2. Ask questions in plain English
3. Get answers from live data in seconds

No dashboards. No warehouse. No analyst required for basic questions.

## What you can ask

"Show me revenue by region, compare to last quarter, flag anything below plan."

"Which customers are at risk of churning? What's the common pattern?"

"What happens to cash flow if we delay hiring by one quarter?"

These are the questions executives actually ask. They currently take days to answer. With conversational BI, they take seconds.
