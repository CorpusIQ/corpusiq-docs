# CorpusIQ Affiliate Program FAQ

## How much can I earn?

25% recurring commission for 3 full years (36 months). Average referral at $200/month = $50/month to you = $1,800 total per customer over 3 years.

## When do I get paid?

Monthly, through Reditus or FirstPromoter. Commission is calculated on the customer's subscription revenue each month.

## How long is the cookie window?

90 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 90 days, you get credit.

## What if my referral cancels?

Commission stops when they cancel. But the average SaaS customer stays 3+ years — and you earn for every month they're active.

## Can I refer from multiple channels?

Yes. Same link works everywhere — YouTube descriptions, blog posts, newsletters, social media, client emails.

## Do I need to be a CorpusIQ customer?

No. You can promote without using the product — though affiliates who use it typically convert better because they speak from experience.

## What markets are eligible?

Global. CorpusIQ accepts customers worldwide.

## Can agencies or consultants join?

Yes. This is actually the highest-earning channel. If you recommend tools to clients, use your affiliate link. One agency sending 30 referrals at $200/month earns $1,500/month recurring.

## Is there a minimum payout?

Reditus: $50 minimum. FirstPromoter: $100 minimum.

## How do I track performance?

Login to Reditus or FirstPromoter. See clicks, signups, active referrals, and monthly commissions in real time.

## What if I have questions?

Email media@corpusiq.io. Real humans, not bots.
