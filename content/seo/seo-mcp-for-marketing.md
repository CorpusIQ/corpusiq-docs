## The marketing data problem

Every marketing team has the same Wednesday:

1. Pull Google Ads spend and conversions
2. Pull Meta Ads spend and conversions
3. Pull LinkedIn Ads spend (if B2B)
4. Pull Klaviyo campaign revenue
5. Pull GA4 traffic and conversion data
6. Export to a spreadsheet
7. Try to calculate blended ROAS
8. Realize the date ranges don't match
9. Start over

This takes 3-4 hours. Every week. For a single report that answers "are our ads working?"
