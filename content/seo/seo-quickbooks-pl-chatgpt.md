# QuickBooks P&L with ChatGPT — Financial Answers Without Reports

Your P&L lives in QuickBooks. But getting a clear answer means running the report, setting date ranges, and interpreting rows of numbers.

Connect QuickBooks to ChatGPT and ask in plain English:

> "What's our net income this quarter? Compare to last quarter and budget."

> "What drove the margin change? Break it down by revenue and expense categories."

> "Show me expenses growing faster than revenue. Which should I investigate?"

> "What's our profit per customer? Who are our most profitable accounts?"

Setup: corpusiq.io → QuickBooks (read-only, 30s) → ChatGPT → Ask.
