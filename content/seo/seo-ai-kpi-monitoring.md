# AI for KPI Monitoring — Know Your Numbers Before They Know You

KPIs are supposed to tell you when something's wrong. In practice, they tell you something was wrong three weeks ago — when the monthly report finally lands.

Here's how to monitor your KPIs in real time.

## The KPI monitoring problem

Every company has KPIs: MRR, churn rate, CAC, LTV, pipeline coverage, burn rate. Every company checks them monthly. By the time you see a problem, it's been festering for weeks.

A SaaS company losing 1% more customers per month than usual might not notice for 90 days. By then, they've lost 3% more revenue than they should have.

## How MCP changes KPI monitoring

Connect your tools. Then ask daily:

> "What's our MRR today vs 7 days ago? Any customers churned that shouldn't have?"

> "What's our pipeline coverage for this quarter? Is it above 3x?"

> "What's our burn rate and runway at current spending?"

> "Which KPIs are outside normal range? Flag anything anomalous."

Live data. Every day. Not once a month.
