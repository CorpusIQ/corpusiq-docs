# MCP for Trucking — Fleet Metrics, Costs, and Customer Data

Trucking companies run: TMS for loads, QuickBooks for costs, Stripe for billing, HubSpot for shipper pipeline, and ELD for driver logs.

Every dispatch meeting requires pulling from five systems.

## How MCP unifies fleet ops

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "What's our revenue per mile this week? Break down by lane and customer."

> "Which trucks are due for maintenance? Show me the schedule and cost estimate."

> "What's our deadhead percentage? Which lanes have the best backhaul rates?"

> "Which customers have the highest volume? What's their on-time performance?"
