# Best AI Knowledge Platform for Business — What Actually Works

You want your team to ask questions and get answers from company data. You've looked at knowledge base tools, vector search platforms, and RAG frameworks. Most of them require building something.

Here's what actually works in 2026.

## The knowledge platform landscape

Three approaches exist for connecting company data to AI:

**1. Vector-based knowledge bases** (Guru, Notion AI, Glean): Index your documents. Search semantically. Good for static content like policies, wikis, and docs.

**2. Build-your-own RAG** (LangChain + Pinecone + custom pipeline): Maximum flexibility. Requires 2-3 months of engineering and ongoing maintenance.

**3. MCP platforms** (CorpusIQ): Connect live business tools directly. No indexing. No pipelines. Ask questions, get live answers.

## Why MCP beats knowledge bases for business data

Knowledge bases are great for "what's our vacation policy?" They fail for "what's our revenue this month?"

Business data isn't static text. It's live numbers that change by the second. A knowledge base has last month's P&L. MCP has this morning's.

| | Knowledge Base | MCP Platform |
|---|---------------|-------------|
| **Data freshness** | Days to weeks old | Live, every query |
| **Cross-tool queries** | Not possible | Built-in |
| **Numeric/aggregate** | Poor | Native |
| **Setup** | Upload documents | 30-second OAuth |
| **Best for** | Policies, wikis, docs | Revenue, pipeline, KPIs |

## The stack that works

Use a knowledge base for company docs. Use MCP for business data. They're complementary.
