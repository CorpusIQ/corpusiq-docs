# MCP for Law Firms — Billable Hours, Collections, and Client Pipeline

Connect via MCP and ask:

> "What's our realization rate? Billed vs collected by attorney and practice area."

> "Show me unbilled time. Who hasn't submitted hours? What's the total value?"

> "Which matters are approaching their budget? Flag any within 10% of cap."

> "What's our collection rate on AR over 90 days? Which clients need follow-up?"
