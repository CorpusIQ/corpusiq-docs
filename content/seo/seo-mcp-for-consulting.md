# MCP for Consulting Firms — One Question Across Every Client

You manage 10 clients. Each has QuickBooks, HubSpot, and Stripe. That's 30 systems to check.

What if you could ask one question and get answers across all of them?

## The consulting data trap

Consultants bill for strategy. They spend their time on data entry.

Every week: pull QuickBooks reports for 10 clients. Check Stripe for revenue. Check HubSpot for pipeline. Build 10 reports. Each takes 30 minutes. That's 5 hours of data plumbing before any actual consulting happens.

## How MCP changes consulting

Connect each client's tools once. Then ask across all of them:

> "Which clients had their best month ever? What drove it?"

> "Across all clients, which industries are growing fastest?"

> "Which clients are below their revenue target? Who needs attention?"

> "Compare performance across my client portfolio. Rank by growth rate."

One question. Multiple clients. Live data. No reports to build.

## What consultants tell us

> "I used to spend Mondays building client reports. Now I ask questions across my entire portfolio in minutes. My clients get insights, not data dumps. My billable rate doubled because I stopped doing data entry."
