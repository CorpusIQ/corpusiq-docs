# MCP for Professional Services — Billable Hours, Utilization, and Revenue

Professional services firms run: time tracking for billable hours, QuickBooks for invoicing, Stripe for payments, HubSpot for client pipeline, and Gmail for client communications.

Every partner meeting starts with "where are the numbers?"

## How MCP unifies professional services

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "What's our utilization rate this month? Break down by consultant and practice."

> "What's our realization rate? Billed vs collected by client."

> "Show me the pipeline for Q4. Which opportunities need attention?"

> "Which clients have outstanding invoices? Sort by amount and days overdue."

> "What's our revenue per consultant? Who's above and below target?"
