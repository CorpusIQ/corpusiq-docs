# MCP for Property Management — Every Property, Every Metric, One Question

Property managers run: AppFolio/Yardi for property ops, QuickBooks for financials, Stripe for rent collection, HubSpot for tenant pipeline, and Gmail for maintenance coordination.

Every owner report means pulling from five systems per property.

## How MCP unifies property management

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "What's our occupancy rate across all properties? Break down by property."

> "Which tenants haven't paid rent? Show me amount, days late, and unit."

> "What's our NOI by property this month? Compare to budget and last year."

> "Show me all open maintenance requests. Sort by age and urgency."
