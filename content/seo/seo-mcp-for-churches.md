# MCP for Churches and Nonprofits — Donations, Expenses, and Member Data

Churches and religious organizations run: Planning Center for members, QuickBooks for finances, Stripe for online giving, Mailchimp for communications, and HubSpot for visitor follow-up.

Every board meeting requires pulling reports from five systems.

## How MCP unifies church operations

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "What's our total giving this month vs last month? Break down by fund and method."

> "What's our budget vs actuals by ministry? Which areas need attention?"

> "Show me new visitor follow-up status. Who hasn't been contacted?"

> "What's our member engagement trend? Attendance, giving, and small group participation."
