MSPs and IT service providers manage: PSA for tickets, QuickBooks for billing, RMM for infrastructure, HubSpot for client pipeline.

> "Show me all open tickets sorted by age. Which clients have the most unresolved?"

> "What's our SLA compliance this month? Which clients are we failing for?"

> "Which clients have expiring contracts? What's the renewal value at risk?"

> "What's our recurring revenue by client? Which have the best margin?"
