# MCP for Government Contractors — DCAA Compliance, Project Costs, and Revenue

Government contractors manage: Deltek for project accounting, QuickBooks for financials, Stripe for payments, HubSpot for BD pipeline, and SharePoint for contract documents.

DCAA compliance means every number must be traceable across systems.

## How MCP helps government contractors

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "Show me all costs charged to contract #XYZ this quarter. Break down by labor, materials, ODCs."

> "What's our incurred cost submission status? Which contracts need attention?"

> "Show me our BD pipeline by agency. What's the total value by stage?"

> "Which contracts are approaching their period of performance end? What's the revenue at risk?"
