# ChatGPT for QuickBooks — Natural Language Financial Queries

ChatGPT can now query your actual QuickBooks data. Not generic business advice — your real P&L, balance sheet, invoices.

> "What's our net income this quarter?"

> "Show me all unpaid invoices over $5K. Sort by days overdue."

> "What were our top 5 expenses last month? Any unusual items?"

> "Compare this month's revenue to the same month last year."

## How it works

MCP (Model Context Protocol) connects ChatGPT directly to QuickBooks. You authenticate once via OAuth (read-only). ChatGPT can query your financial data but can't modify anything.

No API keys. No developer. 2-minute setup at corpusiq.io.
