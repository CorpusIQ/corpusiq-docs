## The RevOps data fragmentation

RevOps touches every revenue system:

- **HubSpot:** Pipeline, deal stages, win rates, rep performance
- **Stripe:** Actual revenue, churn, expansion revenue
- **QuickBooks:** Invoiced revenue, AR aging, recognized revenue
- **Salesforce:** Forecast, quota attainment, territory performance

Answering "what's our real revenue this quarter?" means pulling from all four and reconciling the differences. The pipeline says $2M. Stripe says $1.8M. QuickBooks says $1.75M. Which one is right?
