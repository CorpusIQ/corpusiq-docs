# MCP for Insurance — Policy Data, Claims, and Financials in One Question

Insurance agencies manage: policy management systems, QuickBooks for commissions, Stripe for premium payments, HubSpot for client relationships, and Gmail for claims correspondence.

Five systems. Zero unified client view.

## The insurance data fragmentation

Every client interaction requires checking multiple systems:

- **Policy system:** Coverage details, renewal dates, claims history
- **QuickBooks:** Commission tracking, agency revenue, carrier payables
- **Stripe:** Premium payments, billing status
- **HubSpot:** Client communications, renewal pipeline
- **Gmail:** Claims correspondence, carrier communications

Answering "how is the Johnson account doing?" means checking all five.

## How MCP unifies the client view

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "Show me everything on the Johnson account — policies, premiums, claims, last contact, and renewal date."

> "Which clients have renewals in the next 30 days? Sort by premium value."

> "What's our agency revenue this month? Break it down by carrier."

> "Which clients have open claims? What's the status of each?"
