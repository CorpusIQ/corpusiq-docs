# MCP for Staffing Agencies — Placements, Margins, and Client Pipeline

Staffing agencies run: Bullhorn for placements, QuickBooks for payroll, Stripe for billing, HubSpot for client pipeline, and Gmail for candidate communications.

Every weekly ops meeting means pulling from five systems to answer "how are we doing?"

## How MCP unifies staffing ops

Connect your tools. Then ask:

> "What's our gross margin by client this month? Who are our most profitable accounts?"

> "Show me current openings vs available candidates. Where are the gaps?"

> "What's our fill rate? Time-to-fill by role type?"

> "Which clients haven't had a placement in 60 days? What's the opportunity?"
