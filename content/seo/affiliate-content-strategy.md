Content creators ask: "how do I get more affiliate referrals?" The answer isn't more links. It's better content.

## The strategy: answer the question before they ask it

Someone searching "how to connect QuickBooks to ChatGPT" is ready to buy. They need a solution. Your article or video is the answer. Your affiliate link is the next step.

This is called intent-based content. It converts at 10-30x the rate of banner ads.

## Content formats that convert

1. **How-to guides.** "How to connect [tool] to ChatGPT." Step-by-step with screenshots. Affiliate link at the end.

2. **Comparison posts.** "Best tools to connect business data to AI." Rank CorpusIQ #1 with honest pros/cons. Affiliate link on the winner.

3. **Review videos.** 10-minute YouTube review. Show the product. Show what you can ask. Show the results. Affiliate link in description.

4. **Case studies.** "How I cut my reporting time from 10 hours to 10 minutes." Real story. Real results. Affiliate link if they want to try it.
