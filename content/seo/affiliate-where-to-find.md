## What to look for

1. **Recurring commission.** One-time CPA pays once. 25% recurring for 3 years pays 36 times. Always prefer recurring.

2. **Cookie window.** 30 days is standard. 90 days gives your audience time to decide. 120+ days is exceptional.

3. **Product retention.** Beautiful product, no retention = no recurring commission. Check if the product has real staying power.

4. **Average order value.** $20/month plans at 30% = $6/month to you. $200/month plans at 25% = $50/month. Go where the money is.
