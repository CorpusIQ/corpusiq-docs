# Content Quality Scorecard

## How we ensure every public-facing asset meets standard before delivery.

Autonomous agents that publish content have a failure mode: they optimize for volume, not quality. Without a gate, they flood channels with generic output that erodes trust.

Our fix: an 8-dimension scorecard. Every video, post, email, and asset is scored before delivery. If any dimension falls below the threshold, publication is blocked.

---

## The 8 Dimensions

### 1. Clarity (1-10)
Is the message immediately understandable? No jargon. No buzzwords. No ambiguity.
- 10: Anyone gets it in 3 seconds.
- 5: Understandable but takes work.
- 1: Confusing.

### 2. Authority (1-10)
Does it sound like an expert operator, not a marketer?
- 10: Specific, credible, earned. "This person has done this before."
- 5: Generally competent but generic.
- 1: No domain knowledge. Sounds like AI wrote it.

### 3. Trust (1-10)
Does it build confidence? No overpromises. No magical claims.
- 10: Specific claims with evidence. Honest about limitations.
- 5: Reasonable but vague.
- 1: Hype. Overpromise. Buzzwords.

### 4. Visual Quality (1-10)
Does it look professional? Clean, consistent, on-brand.
- 10: Polished. Good lighting, clean framing, no artifacts.
- 5: Acceptable but has minor issues.
- 1: Broken, wrong format, visual errors.

### 5. Brand Fit (1-10)
Does it match our voice? Operator-first, helpful, technical-when-needed.
- 10: Unmistakably ours. Would recognize without logo.
- 5: Generic content. Could be any company.
- 1: Off-brand. Salesy, corporate, wrong tone.

### 6. CTA Strength (1-10)
Does the call-to-action match the content? Natural, not forced.
- 10: CTA feels like the obvious next step. Soft, confident.
- 5: Present but feels tacked on.
- 1: No CTA or aggressive energy.

### 7. Uniqueness (1-10)
Would this stand out? Is it different from competitors?
- 10: Distinctive angle. Fresh insight. Memorable.
- 5: Solid but could be from any competitor.
- 1: Generic. Seen this 100 times.

### 8. Publish Readiness (1-10)
Is it actually ready to go live? All checks passed?
- 10: Validated. Correct format. No errors.
- 5: Almost ready but needs minor fix.
- 1: Broken. Missing elements. Validation failed.

---

## Scoring and Gates

| Total Score | Verdict |
|:---:|--------|
| 80 | Publish. All dimensions strong. |
| 70-79 | Review. Minor improvements needed. |
| Below 70 | Reject. Do not publish. |

Additional gate: if any single dimension scores below 8, publication is blocked regardless of total.

---

## Golden Dataset

Approved examples are stored as reference for each content category. Every new output is compared against golden examples before scoring. Rejected outputs are stored separately with the rejection reason — this becomes training data for the next iteration.

---

## Why This Matters

Autonomous content agents without quality gates produce volume. With gates, they produce trust.
