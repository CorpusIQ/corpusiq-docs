# Revenue & Finance Prompts

Ask these in Claude or ChatGPT with CorpusIQ connected.

## Cash Flow Health

> What's blocking our cash flow right now? Show overdue invoices, upcoming bills, and available cash across all accounts.

## Profit & Loss Snapshot

> Give me a P&L for last month. Break down revenue by channel, COGS, and operating expenses. Compare to the prior month.

## Revenue Trends

> Show revenue trend over the last 6 months. Any channels declining? Which product lines are growing fastest?

## Overdue Invoices

> List all invoices past due by more than 15 days. Who owes the most? Flag anyone over 60 days.

## Stripe MRR

> What's our current MRR from Stripe? Break it down by plan, show churn rate, and highlight any accounts at risk.

## Expense Audit

> Show me the top 10 expense categories from QuickBooks this quarter. Any outliers or unexpected charges?

## Custom

Combine any of these questions. CorpusIQ handles multi-source queries natively — just ask.
