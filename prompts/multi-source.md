# Multi-Source Prompts

Ask these in Claude or ChatGPT with CorpusIQ connected. The real power is asking one question that spans Shopify, QuickBooks, Google Ads, Stripe, and HubSpot simultaneously.

## Board Update

> Draft a board update for last quarter. Pull revenue from Stripe and QuickBooks, growth metrics from Shopify, marketing performance from Google Ads, and pipeline from HubSpot. Keep it to one page.

## Investor Update

> Give me an investor update for this month. Show MRR, growth rate, burn rate, cash runway, top 3 wins, and top 3 risks. Pull data from all connected sources.

## Weekly Business Review

> Run a weekly business review. Show revenue vs target, top campaigns, sales pipeline movement, customer support tickets, and any anomalies across all connected tools.

## Cross-Department Report

> Give me a cross-department snapshot: marketing spend and ROAS, sales pipeline and closed deals, product usage metrics, support ticket volume, and finance runway. One page.

## Growth Health Check

> How healthy is our growth? Show new customers, churned customers, net revenue retention, CAC trends, and LTV trends. Pull from Stripe, Shopify, and HubSpot.

## Monthly Operations Review

> Run a monthly ops review: top 5 selling products, churn rate, support ticket trends, overdue invoices, and headcount costs. Compare to last month.

## Anomaly Detection

> Flag anything unusual across all connected tools. Revenue spikes or drops, unusual ad spend, sudden churn, support ticket surges — anything that looks off.

## Custom

Combine any of these questions. CorpusIQ handles multi-source queries natively — just ask.
