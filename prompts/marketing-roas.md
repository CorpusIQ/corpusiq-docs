# Marketing & ROAS Prompts

Ask these in Claude or ChatGPT with CorpusIQ connected.

## True ROAS

> What's my true ROAS by channel this month? Pull ad spend from Google Ads, Meta Ads, and LinkedIn. Pull revenue from Shopify and Stripe. Show blended and per-channel.

## Customer Acquisition Cost

> Calculate my CAC for the last 90 days. Include ad spend, agency fees, and tool costs. Break down by channel.

## Campaign Performance

> Which campaigns performed best this week? Show spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and ROAS for each active campaign across all ad platforms.

## Attribution

> How many sales came from each channel last month? Show first-touch and last-touch attribution using Google Analytics data.

## Email Marketing ROI

> Pull email campaign performance from Klaviyo. Show send volume, open rate, click rate, and revenue per campaign. Compare to last month.

## Funnel Analysis

> Walk me through our marketing funnel from impression to purchase. Where are we losing the most people? What's the biggest bottleneck?

## Budget Allocation

> If I have $50K to spend next month, how should I allocate it across channels based on historical ROAS?

## Custom

Combine any of these questions. CorpusIQ handles multi-source queries natively — just ask.
