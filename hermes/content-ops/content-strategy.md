# Belief Bridge Content Strategy

Content is not product promotion. Content is belief transition.

Every piece of content moves a prospect from their current belief to the belief required to buy CorpusIQ. Product features appear only at the end, earned through insight.

## Core Directive

Never start with CorpusIQ. Always start with the belief the audience currently holds. Build a bridge. Product appears naturally at the end.

## Content Mix

| Type | % | Purpose |
|------|---|---------|
| Problem Awareness | 40 | Surface the hidden cost of the current belief |
| Problem Reframing | 30 | Show the problem is bigger than they think |
| Category Creation | 20 | Define the new category |
| Product | 10 | CorpusIQ as the logical solution |

## Reject Content That

- Starts with CorpusIQ features
- Sounds like a product brochure
- Mentions AI without a business problem
- Fails to challenge an existing belief
- Lists connector counts as the main hook
- Uses em dashes

## Master Belief Bridge

**Current belief:** AI adoption is the goal.

**Target belief:** Trusted knowledge infrastructure is the prerequisite.

## Belief Shift Library

| From | To |
|------|----|
| We have data | We lack intelligence |
| Search works | Retrieval is broken |
| AI needs documents | AI needs governed knowledge |
| Dashboards solve this | Asking questions solves this |
| More AI tools | Better knowledge infrastructure |

## Content Generation Template

Before writing, answer:

1. What belief does the audience currently hold?
2. What belief should they hold after reading?
3. What insight creates the bridge?
4. What business consequence supports it?
5. How does CorpusIQ naturally fit at the end?

## Content Ontology Gate

Every piece runs through the 6-dimension ontology gate before publishing:

1. Public vs Internal — no internal metrics
2. Human vs AI — no em dashes, no AI cliches
3. Audience — right tone for platform
4. Naming — "Corpus IQ" two words
5. Help-First — standalone value without product mention
6. Freshness — no repeats within 48 hours

## Content Tracker

All posts logged to `content-tracker.jsonl` with type tags. Weekly review compares belief-bridge vs feature content performance.
