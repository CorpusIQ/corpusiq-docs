# MCP Server Integration Guides

CorpusIQ connects to 30+ MCP servers providing data access across analytics, payments, CRM, marketing, and operations. Each server has a dedicated integration guide covering setup, tools, common queries, and best practices.

## Analytics and SEO

### Google Analytics 4
Access web traffic, sessions, users, conversions, and real-time visitors. Supports custom reports with dimensions and metrics. Use for traffic analysis, conversion tracking, and audience insights.

### Google Search Console
Query search performance data: clicks, impressions, CTR, position. Inspect URLs for index status. Manage sitemaps. Use for SEO monitoring and content optimization.

### Ahrefs
Domain rating, backlink analysis, organic keywords, competitor research. Superior backlink data vs Semrush. Use for SEO competitive analysis and link building.

### Semrush
Domain overview, organic and paid keywords, competitor analysis. Use for keyword research and competitive benchmarking.

### PostHog
Product analytics: events, persons, HogQL queries, funnel analysis. Use for product usage tracking and user behavior analysis.

## Payments and Finance

### Stripe
Complete payment operations: charges, customers, payouts, balance transactions, refunds, disputes. Use for revenue reporting and payment reconciliation.

### QuickBooks
Financial accounting: profit and loss, invoices, balance sheet, accounts receivable/payable. Use for financial reporting and cash flow analysis.

## CRM and Marketing

### HubSpot
Contacts, deals, companies, pipelines. Use for sales pipeline reporting and CRM management.

### ActiveCampaign
Email marketing and CRM: contacts, lists, campaigns, automations, deals. Use for email marketing operations and lead nurturing.

### Klaviyo
Email and SMS marketing: campaigns, flows, abandoned cart, list growth, revenue attribution. Use for ecommerce email performance.

### Mailchimp
Email campaigns, lists, subscribers, open rates, click rates, audience growth. Use for newsletter operations and audience analytics.

## Ecommerce

### Shopify
Orders, products, customers, inventory. Use for ecommerce operations and revenue tracking.

### Amazon Seller
Orders, inventory, sales metrics, seller performance. Use for marketplace operations.

### eBay
Orders, transactions, seller standards, traffic reports. Use for marketplace performance.

## Social and Content

### YouTube
Channel analytics, video performance, transcripts, comments. Use for content performance and engagement tracking.

### TikTok
Account analytics, video performance, engagement metrics. Use for short-form content measurement.

### LinkedIn Ads
Sponsored ad accounts, campaigns, creatives, daily performance analytics. Use for B2B advertising reporting.

### Meta Ads
Facebook and Instagram advertising: campaigns, ad sets, ads, audience insights. Use for social advertising analytics.

## Communication

### Slack
Channels, messages, threads, files, workspace analytics. Use for team communication monitoring.

### Calendar (Google + Outlook)
Events, availability, scheduling. Use for meeting management and availability checks.

## Database

### PostgreSQL / MSSQL / MongoDB / Cosmos DB
Direct database queries, table listing, schema inspection. Use for custom data analysis and reporting.

## Cross-Source Analysis

CorpusIQ's cross-source connectors correlate data across platforms: Google Ads spend vs GA4 sessions, Klaviyo email vs Shopify revenue, multi-channel attribution summaries. This is the unique capability that turns individual data sources into unified business intelligence.
