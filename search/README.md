# Search

Search across your connected business tools with natural language.

## How Search Works

CorpusIQ search queries your live data across all connected sources simultaneously. Unlike traditional search that indexes stored copies, CorpusIQ routes your question to the relevant APIs and returns cited results in seconds.

## Supported Searches

- **Cross-source queries** — "Show me all customers who bought from both Shopify and Stripe"
- **Natural language filtering** — "Which campaigns had ROAS over 3x last month?"
- **Date-range comparisons** — "Compare Q2 revenue this year vs last year"
- **Entity lookups** — "Find the invoice for customer Acme Corp"
